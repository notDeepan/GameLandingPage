Valorant is a free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for Windows.

Call of Duty: Warzone was a free-to-play battle royale video game developed by Raven Software and Infinity Ward, and published by Activision.

Horizon Forbidden West is a 2022 action role-playing game developed by Guerrilla Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment.

Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale-hero shooter game developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts.

The Finals is an upcoming first-person shooter, developed and published by Nexon subsidiary Embark Studios.
